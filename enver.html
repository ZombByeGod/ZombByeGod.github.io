Welcome To Enver Creek

Enver Creek is a school. They teach all sort of subjects, from math to media art, you won't find
another school in surrey with such a variety of choices to choose from.


This school use a A to D block systems. Your class last for about 1 and a half hours and the order of
each class is different every day. Enver Creek's starts school at 8:30 and closes at 2:53 for
students, but it's different on wednesday's.
